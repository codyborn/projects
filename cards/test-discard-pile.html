Discard Pile Feature Test

Test Instructions

Open the main game at http://localhost:8000 and test the following features:

  1. Discard Pile UI: Check that the discard pile area appears to the right of the deck
  2. Right-click to Discard: Right-click on any card to discard it to the discard pile
  3. Drag to Discard: Drag cards near the discard pile to see the gravity well effect
  4. Multi-card Selection: Click and drag on empty table area to select multiple cards
  5. Group Dragging: After selecting cards, drag any selected card to move the group
  6. Shuffle Button: Hover over the discard pile counter to see the shuffle button
  7. Counter Updates: Verify the discard pile counter updates when cards are added/removed
